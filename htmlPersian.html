Breakfast

Adasi

Making Adasi for 5 people and the vital material
200 g lentils for 5 people
Rice 50 g
1 liter of water
1. Wet the lentils
2- We make hot onions
3. Pour the lentils in the pressure cooker
4. Add the hot onion
5. Add a quarter of the rice to the lentils
6. Add a liter of water, then let it settle.

Lunch

Kalam polo

orders and the vital materials for making kalam polo shiraz
Minced meat 300 g
one white cabbage
One kilo of rice

1- First, soak the rice
2- Then peel the kale and chop them finely. Chop the cleaned vegetables for the cabbage.
3. Fry the chopped words in a suitable pan with some oil.
4. In a suitable pot, pour some water and half a tablespoon of turmeric jam, and after the water reaches boiling temperature, add the rice to the boiling water, and after a few minutes, drain the rice. Then put the desired crease in the bottom of the pot and mix the rice with cabbage and vegetables and pour in the pot.
5. Cover the pot with a saucepan and place on the pot and turn down the heat to simmer the rice. In the next step, pour the minced meat in a suitable dish and then grate the onion on the meat with fine ribs and add chickpea flour along with pepper, turmeric and salt to the meat.
6. Knead the minced meat mixture well. Then cut the minced meat mixture into small dumplings. Pour some oil in a suitable pan and fry the meatballs in oil. Then place the fried meatballs in the middle of the pilaf to cook with the pilaf.

Dinner

Kashkeh bademjan

orders for making kashkeh bademjan
Eggplant 5 pcs
4 walnuts
Onion 1 clove garlic 5 cloves
Whey 40 grams of salt and pepper as needed
Dried mint 2 tbsp
Oil as needed

1. Peel the eggplants, then fry.
2. Fry the onions in the same pan with a little oil until they are completely golden.
4. Add the fried eggplants to the pan again.
5. Mix the eggplants with the other ingredients, then add half a cup of water to the pan and let it cook completely with the lid closed. After the eggplants are fully cooked, mash them with the back of a spoon.
6. Add chopped walnuts and curd to them.
7- Pour the eggplant curd in a serving dish and garnish it with curd, hot onion and hot mint.
